About the Centre

Our Mission

Al Khaleel Dawah Centre is dedicated to nurturing faith, learning and community well‑being. The new centre will provide spaces for prayer, education, youth programmes and community support services.

Planned Centre Design

Our Vision

To establish a welcoming, sustainable space that inspires unity, learning and service. Programmes will include Qur'an and Arabic classes, youth mentorship, food relief and community outreach.

Project Phases

From Groundwork to Grand Opening

Phase 1 — Foundation & Site Prep

Land preparation, permits, utilities and foundational work to prepare the site for construction.

Phase 2 — Structure & Roofing

Primary structure, roofing and shell to bring the building to life.

Phase 3 — Interiors & Services

Prayer hall finishes, classrooms, ablution facilities, electrical, HVAC and accessibility features.

Phase 4 — Community Areas & Landscaping

Courtyard, green spaces and multi‑purpose rooms for community programmes and events.

Final Vision — Completed Centre

A welcoming, functional and beautiful space serving the community every day.
